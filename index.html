Your phone could be a crime scene

Cell Phone Strap as a Police line

According to the Korea Communications Commission's 2021 Survey(released in April 2022), 1.5 out of 10 adults and 3 out of 10 teens experienced cyberbullying. Therefore, I came up with a police line cell phone strap to warn people that their comments and expressions left on the internet could be a cyberbullying to someone.

  • The shape of the cell phone strap inspired me to use the police line as the strap to express the message I wanted to convey
  • You can use "Strap Crime Scene" by attaching the strap to the cell phone case
